Being part of our community means understanding the impact of your actions and engaging in meaningful interactions that contribute positively to the platform and its users.

We strictly prohibit behavior that significantly disrupts the experience of others, such as:

- Posting off-topic comments
- Excessively liking or following users or tags within a short period of time
- Utilizing platform features in a way that generates excessive notifications for other users
- Misusing any other platform feature to cause disruption

Please be aware that these actions may also violate other restrictions outlined in our [Acceptable Use Policies](/legal/use-policies/general), potentially amounting to bullying and harassment depending on the nature and severity of the activity.
