Posting content that distorts reality is strictly prohibited if it is inaccurate, false (misinformation), or intentionally deceptive (disinformation), particularly when it can harm the public or impede fair and equal participation in a free and open society. Examples of such content include:

- Inaccurate or unsupported medical claims that pose risks to public health and safety
- Manipulated audio or visual media likely to mislead and harm the public interest
- False or misleading content that hinders individuals' engagement in civic activities
- Unsubstantiated claims promoting hate or targeted harassment against specific groups

While we encourage active participation in sharing ideas, perspectives, and experiences, personal accounts and observations may not be subject to dispute. Parody and satire that align with our [Acceptable Use Policies](/legal/use-policies/general) are generally permitted, and we consider the context in which information is presented and understood.

When evaluating content under this policy, Storiny takes into account various factors that help clarify its accuracy, such as clear disclaimers, citations to credible sources, and additional details that provide clarity and reliability.
