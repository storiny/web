## Username Squatting Policy

Account name squatting is strictly prohibited by Storiny. Account names cannot be reserved or passively held with the intention of future use. Any accounts found to violate this policy may be subjected to immediate removal or renaming without prior notice. Additionally, the sale, purchase, or solicitation of account names in exchange for monetary compensation or any other form of remuneration is strictly prohibited and may result in permanent account termination.
