Storiny account names are allocated on a precedence basis, with the principle of first come, first served, and are designated for immediate and continued use. If the desired username has already been used, it is advisable to explore alternative names or creative variations. The use of numerical characters, underscores, or uncommon spellings may assist in identifying an available and appealing username.

Should there be a perceived infringement of trademark rights by another account holder, additional details regarding the submission of a trademark complaint can be accessed through the [Trademark Policy](/legal/policies/trademark) page.

## Username Squatting Policy

Account name squatting is strictly prohibited by Storiny. Account names cannot be reserved or passively held with the intention of future use. Any accounts found to violate this policy may be subjected to immediate removal or renaming without a prior notice. Additionally, the sale, purchase, or solicitation of account names in exchange for monetary compensation or any other form of remuneration is strictly prohibited and may result in permanent account termination.
