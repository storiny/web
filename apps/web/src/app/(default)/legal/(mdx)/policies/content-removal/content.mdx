We recognize that copyrighted, trademarked, or private content may intentionally be published on Storiny. Given the diverse nature of such content and the varying laws that apply, each category has its own distinct reporting requirements that are clearly outlined in our policies.

If you wish to request the removal of content from Storiny, we kindly request that you familiarize yourself with each of these policies and carefully review their respective reporting requirements before submitting a report. In the event that we receive an incomplete report, it will be necessary for us to seek clarifications or revisions, requiring you to resubmit an updated report.

Please be aware that we are unable to assist you in determining which policy is applicable to your specific situation. If, after reviewing the policies provided below, you still have questions regarding whether certain content should be reported as copyright or trademark, we strongly recommend seeking guidance from independent legal counsel.

## DMCA Takedown Policy

Our [DMCA Takedown Policy](/legal/policies/dmca) serves as a means to report any content that you suspect infringes upon a copyright owned by you or your organization.

## Storiny Trademark Policy

The [Storiny Trademark Policy](/legal/policies/trademark) enables you to report any content that utilizes your company or business name, logo, or other trademark-protected materials in a manner that could potentially mislead or confuse others regarding brand or business affiliation.
