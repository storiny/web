Under specific situations, you are allowed to include Storiny logos on your website or third-party application. To get comprehensive details and specific instructions about how to use the logo, we advise you to a look at the [Storiny Logos and Usage page](/branding).

Furthermore, you have the choice to use the Storiny logo on your website for the purpose of connecting to your Storiny account, as long as the utilization of the Storiny logo does not symbolize your company or product.
